Submit a Story or Blog Draft

Share a procedural lesson, resource idea, or blog draft that may help self-represented people better understand civil court procedure.

Submission Guidelines

  • Focus on civil court procedure, organization, deadlines, forms, court access, filing, service, or motion practice.
  • Write in plain language for people who may be new to court procedure.
  • Do not include confidential facts, private identifying information, active case strategy, or legal advice.
  • Accepted upload formats: DOC, DOCX, and PDF.
  • Submissions may be edited, declined, or held for later review.

Please keep a copy of anything you submit. Submissions are reviewed before any possible publication.
