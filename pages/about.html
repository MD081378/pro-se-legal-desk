About

Helping people understand the procedure before the paperwork.

Pro Se Legal Desk is an informational website for people who are learning how civil court procedure works when they are representing themselves or considering self-representation.

What We Do

We organize plain-language information about court selection, complaint structure, filing steps, service, docket access, response deadlines, motion practice, and calendar planning.

What We Do Not Do

We do not provide legal advice, evaluate claims, recommend strategy, represent anyone, or create an attorney-client relationship.

How to Use This Site

Use this site as a starting point, then verify all rules, forms, fees, deadlines, and court procedures with official court sources or a qualified legal professional.
